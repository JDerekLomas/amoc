### Physics Changes to `v4-physics/index.html`
1. **Salinity field** — surface + deep, stacked in GPU temp buffers at offset NX*NY
2. **Density EOS** — ρ(T,S) = ρ₀(1 - αΔT + βΔS) in both vorticity equations
3. **Freshwater → salinity** — no longer cools temperature (was physically backwards)
4. **Deep buoyancy forcing** — +αT·∂Td/∂x in deep vorticity equation
5. **Ice-albedo gradient** — ramps 45-65° with latitude-dependent strength
6. **Stratification check** — deep water forms only when surface denser than deep
7. **3-belt wind curl** — cos(3φ) with SH 2x boost, polar damping
8. **Red-Black SOR** — replaces Jacobi Poisson solver (~4x faster convergence)
9. **Real ETOPO1 bathymetry** — `bathymetry_1deg.json` (ocean depth + land elevation)
10. **Observed initialization** — NOAA SST + WOA23 deep temp from frame 1
11. **Island fix** — land-ocean heat exchange scaled by ocean fraction
12. **New views** — Salinity, Density buttons
13. **New sliders** — Deep Water Formation, Solar Constant
14. **AMOC display** — numeric value with sign
