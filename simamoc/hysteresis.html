About this experiment

This is a JAX-computed quasi-static sweep of freshwater forcing on the spatial coupled ocean-atmosphere-ice model. Each frame is one segment of the ramp; the full loop runs in ~3.5 minutes on a Mac laptop.

The maps on top are surface fields at the model’s state for the segment you’re viewing. The curve is the AMOC strength as a function of meltwater. The two not overlapping = bistability = the AMOC has memory.

References: Stommel 1961, Cessi 1994, Rahmstorf 1996, van Westen 2024, Ditlevsen & Ditlevsen 2023. Caveats: limitations doc.
