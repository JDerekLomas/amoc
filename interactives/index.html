Interactives

Each piece of the simulator gets its own small explorable. Play with one knob at a time so the physics and math behind the full amoc-jax simulator become legible. Browser-native, no installs, no server.

Available now

01
Stommel two-box AMOC live
The minimum model that produces an AMOC bifurcation. Two boxes, two equations, two stable branches. Drag F slowly; watch the hysteresis loop trace.

Planned

02
Sverdrup balance todo
Wind curl → meridional transport. Set β, paint a curl pattern, watch transport emerge.
03
Munk boundary layer todo
Why western boundaries intensify. Slider for viscosity A and β. Watch δ_M = (A/β)^{1/3} live.
04
Poisson playground todo
Paint a vorticity field ζ; the FFT solver inverts to streamfunction ψ in real time.
05
Arakawa Jacobian todo
Tracer advection by a velocity field. Show energy and enstrophy conservation under the 9-point form.
06
Coriolis & β todo
A particle on a rotating sphere. Inertial circles. β-drift of vortices.
07
Convective adjustment todo
Cool a column from the top; unstable density profile mixes instantly. Visualize T(z) and S(z).
08
Energy balance / ice-albedo todo
1-D Budyko-Sellers with snowball/ice-free bistability. Slider for solar constant.
09
Rossby waves todo
Disturbance propagates westward. Set the wavelength, watch the dispersion.
10
Equation of state todo
T, S sliders → density. Why cold + salty sinks; a tour of the linear EOS used in the simulator.
Each interactive is self-contained — browser-native, single HTML file, no server. Source in interactives/. The full simulator is in amoc-jax/.
